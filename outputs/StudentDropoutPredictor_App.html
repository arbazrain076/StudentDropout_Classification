📖 About This System

This AI system predicts whether a student is at risk of dropping out using Naive Bayes classification. Trained on 10,000 students with 25 features including academic performance (GPA, attendance), lifestyle factors (study hours, stress), and support systems (scholarship, parental education). The model handles class imbalance (76.5% retained / 23.5% dropout) using class_weight='balanced' and achieves ROC-AUC of 0.812, indicating strong discriminative power.

Why Naive Bayes wins: Despite the "naive" assumption of feature independence, Naive Bayes performs well on this imbalanced dataset due to its probabilistic nature and effective handling of binary classification. It achieves the highest F1 score (0.5812) by balancing precision and recall on the minority class (dropout).
